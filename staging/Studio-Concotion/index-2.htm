Matic Degree

The Leading Office Furniture Supplier

Welcome to the leading Office Furniture Supplier and Office Furniture System in Malaysia. Matic is an office furniture manufacturing company that is located in Malaysia with its headquarters in Kuala Lumpur. With an experience of over 30 years in manufacturing and designing office furniture, its 30 products showrooms are currently on display worldwide. Matic is characterized by production of Eco-friendly products that are not only affordable but are also of high quality. When it comes to office furniture, Matic should be your most preferred choice of them all. This is because, our productsare manufactured from recyclable materials while still maintaining the products quality.

We also have a variety of all office furniture and layouts that are uniquely combined technologically with much functionality as well as simplicity. If you want an experience of products that meet the standards of BIFMA and are fully certified by SGS as well as Green Guard, try shopping with Matic. Matic products include; office chairs, conference and occasional tables, cabinets, office accessories and work tools, panel systems among many other products.
